Artificial Intelligence - ChatBot

As we all know AI or "Artificial Intelligence" had hit the market since few years very much. So, now it has became a curiosity among the tech enthusiast to learn and know more about this topic.
So, the first thing that comes to our mind is what is AI? AI stands for Artificial Intelligence by the name it indicates the "Intelligence" that is "Artificially" generated.
So, in simple words AI is a field of computer science that deals with making machines intelligent so that they can think like a human being.
AI is an emerging trend for today and is doing great in many fields from medical sectors to the industrial sectors and many more.

>

Application of AI:-

Let's talk about one of the applications of AI which is widely used now a days:-
In this blog I will be covering a very exciting application of AI i.e;

"The ChatBot"


A ChatBot is an Artificial Intelligence software that can stimulate a conversation with a user in natural language through messaging applications, websites etc.
Today almost every sectors right from online teaching platforms to banking sectors and many more are using this application of AI to provide a better, easy and efficient method for interaction between human and machine.

There are basically two steps in which ChatBot works:-

The two steps are:-


  1. User Request Analysis.
  2. Returning the response.

User Request Analysis

In this step, the ChatBot analysis the user request to know the user intent and extract the relevant entities to provide a best solution. It is very important and core step because without input you will not be able to produce any result.

Returning the response

Now, when the user's purpose is being identified by the chatbot, it's time for the most appropriate response.

    The responses may be:-
  1. a generic and predefined text.
  2. data stored in enterprise system and many more.....

Importance of ChatBot:-

Now, talking about the chatbot's importance, I will point out the most ones;

  1. Enhancing customer experience,
  2. Customers engagement and operational efficiency.
"Inspite of everything a machine provides,a human intervention will always play a important role and will be a reason behind making any machine intelligent to think like a human being."
